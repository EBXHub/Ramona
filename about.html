About

Welcome to the Balkan’s first marketplace for independent brands!

At Velvet Central, we live for independent brands. We’ve made it our mission to provide a platform for Balkan designers to be able to share their work with an international audience who love to support unique and independent brands. We focus on showing the world some of the best independent brands that the Balkans have to offer.

The Balkans are full of creative and progressive artists which are not given regional support. Therefore, through our site, we decided to give the support needed in order for these branded to be recognized on the international stage. Whatever item you order will be shipped to you directly from the business which has designed and made your product.
